Privacy Policy

USDT Signal ("the App", "we", "our") respects your privacy. This policy describes the data we collect, how we use and store it, the partners who assist us, and the choices available to you.

Summary: We use your device information and analytics data only to deliver trading strategy services and improve the app experience. We do not collect personal information, biometric identifiers, or sell personal data.

1. Data We Collect

  • Device & Analytics: basic device metadata, crash logs, and analytics events collected via Firebase Analytics/Crashlytics to maintain service quality and improve the app experience.
  • Advertising ID: Google AdMob may collect your advertising ID for personalized ad delivery. This information is used only for advertising purposes, not for user identification.
  • In-app Purchase Records: purchase receipts and transaction identifiers processed through Apple/Google to unlock ad-free access.

2. How We Use Data

  • Provide real-time USDT price analysis, exchange rate information, and kimchi premium calculations.
  • Deliver AI-based trading strategies and buy/sell signals.
  • Display ads through Google AdMob to support free access to the app.
  • Manage ad-free purchase status and subscription records.
  • Improve app stability through crash reports and analytics.

3. Sharing & Processors

We do not sell personal data. We share information only with trusted processors necessary to operate the App:

  • Google: AdMob for advertising, Firebase services (Analytics, Crashlytics) for app analytics and stability.
  • Apple/Google: process in-app purchases and maintain subscription receipts for ad-free access.

Trading strategy data and user preferences are stored locally on your device. We do not transmit personal information to third parties.

4. Retention & Deletion

  • Analytics and crash logs are retained according to Firebase default lifetimes (up to 26 months) and aggregated thereafter.
  • Purchase records are managed by Apple/Google and retained according to their respective policies.
  • Deletion requests: contact gunnylove@gmail.com and we will assist you with data removal requests.

5. Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your information by emailing us.
  • Withdraw optional permissions such as advertising tracking through your device settings.
  • Cancel ad-free subscriptions through your Apple ID or Google Play account settings.

6. Children

The App is intended for general use. We do not knowingly collect data from children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required by local law).

7. Security

We protect data with encrypted network connections (HTTPS), secure data transmission, and regular monitoring of backend services.

8. Contact

Email: gunnylove@gmail.com
