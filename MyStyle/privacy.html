Privacy Policy

Style Me Glow ("the App", "we", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

1. Data We Collect

  • Account Information via Sign in with Apple / Google Sign-In: name (first/last), email, and profile image. Note: Apple provides name/email only at first authorization or when you choose to share them.
  • Profile & App Data: nickname, gender, age and settings you enter in the app.
  • Region / Location (optional): used to automatically register your region in your profile. Requested only while using the app and not tracked in the background.
  • Device & Diagnostics: basic device info and crash logs to ensure service quality.
  • Push Tokens: FCM/APNs device token to deliver notifications.
  • Advertising: If AdMob is enabled, Google may collect an advertising identifier for ad delivery and fraud prevention.

2. How We Use Data

  • Account creation, authentication, and profile setup
  • Provide features such as AI style analysis, face analysis, and fashion recommendations
  • Regional personalization (when you allow location)
  • Customer support and service improvement
  • Push notifications you opt into
  • Show ads (when using AdMob)

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following processors only to provide our service:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, Firestore/Storage, Cloud Messaging
  • Google Sign-In and Sign in with Apple for authentication
  • Google AdMob (if enabled): advertising

Data is not sold. We do not share personal data with third parties beyond these processors unless required by law.

4. Data Retention & Deletion

  • We retain data while your account is active. When you delete your account in the app or request deletion, we delete or anonymize your personal data within a reasonable period.
  • You may request deletion at any time: gunnylove@gmail.com.

5. Your Rights

  • Access, correct, or delete your information
  • Withdraw consent for optional permissions (e.g., location) via device settings
  • Opt out of personalized ads via system settings (iOS Limit Ad Tracking)

6. Children

The App is not directed to children under the minimum age required by local law. We do not knowingly collect their data.

7. Security

We apply industry-standard protections including encryption in transit and access controls in Firebase.

8. Contact

Email: gunnylove@gmail.com

Effective date: 2025-08-25
